Home Harmony

Home Harmony helps beginners with creating the home that fits them best. Our mission is to make sure that beginners don't feel overwhelmed with a new space; they should want to make the space their HOME not a house. Users can simply take a photo of their space, and the app scans it to provide the user with tons of different options to tailor their living space to their tastes.
